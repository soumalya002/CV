About the Book Fair

The Kolkata International Book Fair 2022 was held at the newly developed Biswa Bangla Mela Prangan. Known for its cultural richness, the fair attracted publishers, authors, and book lovers from across India and the world. It is not just a commercial event but a grand cultural festival celebrating literature, ideas, and intellectual exchange.

Key Highlights

New Venue

The 2022 edition was organized at Biswa Bangla Mela Prangan, offering better infrastructure and accessibility.

International Participation

Publishers and authors from various countries participated, making it a truly global literary event.

Cultural Programs

The fair hosted seminars, book launches, poetry readings, and cultural performances throughout the event.
